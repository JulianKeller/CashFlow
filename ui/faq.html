FAQ Member Support General Questions
Your site isn't displaying correctly for me, why?
A: We only officially support current releases of Mozilla Firefox, Google Chrome, and Apple Safari platforms. We do not officially support nor recommend Microsoft Edge or Internet Explorer.

How do I create a new budget?
A: After you signed in, click the button to create a new category.

Is there any way to add to a category?
A: Yes! All you have to do is in the top-right corner of the category, you click the link and it will prompt you for the new item.
